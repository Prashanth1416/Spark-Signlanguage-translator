Overview
❗
Problem Statement
Millions rely on sign language for daily communication, yet most public systems, institutions, and digital services lack real-time sign interpretation. This creates an invisible but persistent barrier between the deaf community and the hearing world.
💡
Our Solution
SPARK uses a webcam and deep learning to detect hand gestures, classify them into words, translate them into multiple languages, and generate speech — all in real time, entirely browser-based, with no special hardware needed.
Details
⚡
Key Features
  • Real-time two-hand sign recognition
  • 5-frame prediction smoothing
  • Multi-language translation & TTS
  • High accuracy MLP model
  • Wrist-relative coordinate normalization
  • Confidence-based gesture confirmation
