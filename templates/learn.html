Tips for Better Learning
💡
Good Lighting
Ensure your hands are well-lit with the light facing you, not behind you.
📏
Camera Distance
Keep your hands 30–80cm from the camera so all 21 landmarks are visible.
✋
Hold the Sign
Hold each sign steady for at least 1 second for the AI to confirm the gesture.
🔄
Practice Daily
Even 10 minutes a day builds strong muscle memory and sign recognition speed.
